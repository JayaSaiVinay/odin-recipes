Chicken Curry

Chicken curry or curry chicken is a dish originating from the Indian subcontinent.

It is common in the Indian subcontinent, Southeast Asia, Great Britain, and the Caribbean.

It's pretty easy to make and tastes really good!

Ingredients

  • Skinless,boneless chicken breasts
  • Garam masala
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Tomato
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Fresh minced garlic and ginger

Steps

  1. Start by partially cooking your seasoned chicken breasts in a large pan, removing them after they're browned.
  2. After setting the chicken breasts aside, cook garlic, onions, and ginger in the same pan to create the flavor base.
  3. Once the onions are translucent, add in spices, tomatoes, yogurt, and chicken to cook thoroughly.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes for a wow-worthy chicken curry. Finish with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon.
